About Happy Sushi

Happy Sushi is open at 2021. The aim of Happy Sushi is to provide delicious Shushi in Malaysia and satisfy customer wants also make sure that all of the customer can eat the best sushi in Malaysia.

Tasty Japonica Rice
The rice we serve is prepared and cooked the traditional Japanese way to deliver exceptional taste and texture. The shari (rice ball) for sushi is seasoned with premium sushi vinegar and mixed gently for extra deliciousness in each bite of sushi.
Fresh, air-flown salmon from Japan
The secret to delicious Japan Salmon lies in its origins. Bred and nurtured with lots of tender loving care in the clear waters of Japan, the salmon we serve is air-flown and kept at optimal climate to deliver delicious, fresh taste to your palate.
Fresh, Tasty Prawns
The prawns served come from the biggest prawn producer in the world. This gives promise of quality and freshness at great value.
The Finest Green Tea
The best green tea comes from Shizuoka Prefecture. Blessed with unique terrain, mineral-rich soil, favourable climate and dense coastal fog, these factors all make for delicious, quality green tea that warms the heart and refreshes the soul.
